About I AM XIS.

I AM XIS, formerly known as "XISBEAUTY.K" was birthed during the 2020 Coronavirus Pandemic by our founder, Adekoya Okikiola Christopher.

We desire to improve and revamp your lifestyle with top-quality self-care products to help you live your best life.

Behind The Name

I AM XIS means "I AM CHRIS." Chris's Dad inspired XIS. He constantly substituted it for Chris in his text messages. So, we channeled down our founder's name' origin and meaning to establish who we are. As bearers of Christ, God's Spirit lives in us.

Thus, we're devoted to shining the light of Christ through innovative and creative ideas to create a better YOU.

I AM XIS is committed to providing you with all-encompassing details, tips, and hacks on living a fulfilled life. To achieve this, I AM XIS developed, designed, and launched this platform for your accessibility.
